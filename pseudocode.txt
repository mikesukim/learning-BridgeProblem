
1. Simple Logic Flow

	1) main   - 	mutex&cond init
            	    	launch Schedule
            	  	prepare ingredient for threads(info & vehicle count for each group)
            	  	START TRHEADING
            	  	delay between group
            	  	wait for all threads

	2) thread - Arrive 
             		print arrival
             		update vehicle status of north & south bound
	             	wait until other vehicles in same group to arrive
        	     	restriction logic starts
			mutex lock
			   car
				wait if there trucks waiting
				wait if bridge's direction is not same
				wait if bridge is full
				wait if there is car in bridge and new truck arrived
		   	   truck
				wait if bridge is full
				wait if bridge is not same
 	            	update status of N/S parkingLot
        	     	update status of bridge
			print crossing
			mutex unlock
        	    Cross
             		sleep
             	    Leave
			mutex lock
             		update status of bridge
			print leaving
			   car
				switch bridge's direction if no cars waiting on its direction
			   truck
				switch bridge's direction if no trucks waiting on its opposite direction
			mutex signal
			mutex unlock
